1. Create a quiz


Use Copilot to create a quiz or poll for your team. The poll can be about what you did on the weekend, people’s favourite sports or something to do with work.

Use what you learned in the session to make the prompt clear:

● The goal

● The context

● The source

● The expectations


Then bring the result to your next team meeting!
