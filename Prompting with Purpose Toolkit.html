Key concept to keep in mind


Strong prompts come from clear thinking.

When you clearly deﬁne your goal, context, source and expectations, Copilot produces faster, more relevant and more usable outputs.


The better you guide Copilot, the more time you save and the more conﬁdent you become using AI in your day-to-day work.

Quick recapPrompting

Framework

G C S E


GOAL

What you want Copilot to produce

CONTEXT

Why the task exists and who its for

SOURCE

The information or materials Copilot should use

EXPECTATIONS

The tone, structure, length or format required



Reﬁne outputs through conversation


Prompting is not one step. Strong users continue reﬁning outputs by adjusting focus, depth or style until the content works for their audience.

Keep AI human-led

Always review outputs using three key checks:


  • Accuracy

  • Appropriateness

  • Sensitivity


AI supports your work, but you remain responsible for the ﬁnal result.

Upgrade your prompts

  1. Create a quiz


    Use Copilot to create a quiz or poll for your team. The poll can be about what you did on the weekend, people’s favourite sports or something to do with work.

    Use what you learned in the session to make the prompt clear:

    • The goal

    • The context

    • The source

    • The expectations


      Then bring the result to your next team meeting!


  2. Make your own work easier


    Pick one task you do regularly.

    Write your prompt for that task, including:

    • The goal

    • The context

    • The source

    • The expectations

Save that prompt somewhere you can reuse it.
